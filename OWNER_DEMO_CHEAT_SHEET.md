## 3. Main Value Points (Pitch Core)
* **Better Brand Authority:** Instantly positions Chikini Monie as Akure's elite food destination.
* **Frictionless Digital Menu:** Direct category browsing reduces customer wait times.
* **Zero-Leak Order Flow:** Automatically digitizes orders instead of messy social media typing.
* **Desk Verification Control:** Front desk accepted orders coordinate instantly.
* **Chefs KDS Visibility:** Kitchen staff see active tickets clearly on wall screens.
* **Executive Performance Tracking:** Owner gets real-world metrics oversight from anywhere.
* **Scale-Ready Growth Path:** Modular backend built to add unlimited branches.

---

## 4. Questions to Ask (Before Discussing Price)
1. *"Sir, do you want this platform to remain a preview or become a real live ordering system?"*
2. *"Who on your team will be managing incoming customer orders at the desk?"*
3. *"What official phone and WhatsApp numbers should be connected for order verification?"*
4. *"Which menu list, pricing configurations, and signature dish photos are final?"*
5. *"Do you want to integrate Paystack card bank payments immediately, or start with manual transfers on delivery?"*
6. *"Do you want automated WhatsApp notifications sent to customers, or add that integration later?"*
7. *"Which package package direction fits your current budget and business expansion plans best?"*

---

## 5. Pricing Quick View
* **Option 1: Launch Polish Package** ($400 - $600)
  * Final menu, prices, brand assets loaded + custom domain connected.
* **Option 2: Production Core System** ($900 - $1,200) *(RECOMMENDED)*
  * Hardened database, private staff/kitchen passwords, active desk dispatch.
* **Option 3: Full Operations Platform** ($1,800 - $2,500)
  * Live card/bank payments via Paystack, automated WhatsApp receipts, manager edit tools.
* **Monthly Support & Cloud Hosting:** $100 - $250 / month.

---

## 6. Best Recommendation
* **The Smart Recommendation: Option 2 (Production Core)**
  * It secures staff dashboards with secure passwords, runs a controlled production database setup, and handles orders via manual transfer-on-delivery. It is the most robust, cost-effective launchpad.
* **Option 1:** Choose only if they want a polished website presence before starting digital kitchen ops.
* **Option 3:** Choose if they want a complete digital transformation with automated card payments.
